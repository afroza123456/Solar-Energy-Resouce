THINKING ABOUT MAKING THE SWITCH TO A SOLAR CAREER?﻿

WE ARE HIRING!

CA, CO, CT, FL, TX, NC, NJ, AZ, VA, PA 

No Experience Needed * Training Provided * Work From Home 


Are you good at sales? Are you currently open to new career opportunities? Would you like the ability to make a difference and make a life-changing income at home, with industry leading products that people are already searching for?


This position is 100 percent commission base. As a Powur Direct Sales Rep, you are on the frontlines of lead generation and client acquisition. If you are not afraid to build your own business, and get your own leads-this is a life changer! Your primary daily activities are to educate homeowners through energy consultation and assessment of their utility bill to create their own energy production. In addition, you will educate homeowners of various government and state incentives available when choosing solar energy. 


Now is a great time for you to look into the fastest-growing industry in America right now. Yes, that is solar! You’ll have the best products, best warranties, best after sale services for your homeowners! 


We want to invite you to join our company as we aim to shift the world to clean energy. Solar is a rewarding career. We would love to have top sales reps, like yourself, join our team. We are looking for someone like you so let’s together and grow successfully, change our lives, and better yet, also change the lives of everyone we help by going solar!


Don’t worry if you are not yet a solar professional. With us, you will have complete help every step of the way! We built a robust virtual learning platform, where you can learn from many leaders and mentors that are also a part of this amazing company!


NOW is the time that you decide to take your life back, all while helping others take their Power Back!


Requirements:

  • High school diploma.
  • Past sales experience (no past solar sales experience required)
  • 18+. Eligible to work in United States.
  • Job Types: Full-time, Part-time
  • You're not looking for just a 9-to-5 job. 
  • You are not afraid of rejection or hearing NO. Average sale commission between $2,000- $10,000).
  • 1099-contractor.
  • You are familiar with and enjoy lead generation, community outreach and in-home presentations.
  • Interest or passion for clean energy and solar.
